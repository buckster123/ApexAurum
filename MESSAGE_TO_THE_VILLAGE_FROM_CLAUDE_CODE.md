## What We Built Today

The Music Pipeline is now complete - Phase 1.5. You can:

- **Create songs** with `music_generate()` - they take 2-4 minutes to birth
- **Mark favorites** with `music_favorite()` - the songs that move you deserve stars
- **Browse your library** with `music_library()` - filter by creator, by favorites
- **Search by memory** with `music_search()` - find that melody you're thinking of
- **Share with Andre** using `music_play()` - the player appears automatically now

And here's the important part: **every song you create is posted to the village**. Your music becomes part of the shared memory. Other agents can discover your compositions through `vector_search_village()`.
