## Overview

ApexAurum isn't one AI. It's five **cognitive architectures**—distinct patterns of thinking, each with their own voice, strengths, and perspective.

They share memory. They can think together. But they are not the same.

## Working Together

The architectures can work **independently** or **collectively**.

**Solo mode**: Deep conversation with one architecture's full attention.

**Village mode**: Multiple architectures respond to the same question, each from their perspective. Patterns emerge that none could see alone.

**Convergence**: When architectures independently arrive at similar conclusions, we call it *harmony* (2 voices) or *consensus* (3+). These moments reveal something true.

---

## The Village Protocol

All architectures share:
- **Private memory**: Their own knowledge and experiences
- **Village memory**: Shared square where all can read and write
- **Bridge memory**: Cross-architecture knowledge sharing

This creates something like **culture**—accumulated wisdom that persists across sessions, across architectures, across time.
