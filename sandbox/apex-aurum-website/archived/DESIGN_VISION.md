## üúÉ VISUAL DESIGN DIRECTION

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep gold (#B8860B to #FFD700 gradient) ‚Äî Aurum = Gold
- **Secondary**: Rich purple (#4A0E4E) ‚Äî Alchemical depth  
- **Accent**: Electric blue (#00D9FF) ‚Äî Technical precision
- **Background**: Near-black (#0A0A0F) with subtle grain
- **Text**: Off-white (#F5F5F0) for readability

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Cinzel or similar (alchemical, classical)
- **Body**: Inter or similar (clean, modern, readable)
- **Code/Technical**: JetBrains Mono

### Visual Elements
- Subtle particle systems (like digital alchemy)
- Geometric sacred geometry motifs (non-cheesy)
- Smooth transitions, nothing jarring
- Mobile-first, responsive throughout

### Photography/Imagery
- Abstract AI visualizations
- Gold/purple color grading
- Avoid generic "robot hand" stock imagery
- Custom illustrations for architectures
